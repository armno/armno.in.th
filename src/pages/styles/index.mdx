<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Typography" target="_blank">Typography</a> plays a crucial role in web design, serving as both a functional and aesthetic element. Well-chosen fonts and proper text formatting enhance readability and user experience. When designing websites, it's important to maintain consistent typography across different sections while ensuring adequate contrast between text and background colors.

Line height, letter spacing, and paragraph margins all contribute to creating a pleasant reading experience. Additionally, responsive typography that adapts to different screen sizes has become essential in our mobile-first world.
